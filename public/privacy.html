Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What TimeZoneMeet does

TimeZoneMeet lets you search for a city and see the current local time and time zone. Your search text is sent to our servers only to perform that lookup.

Data we process

  • City search: The city name (and optional country code) you enter is sent to our API to find a match and return time zone information. We do not require an account for basic use.
  • Premium purchases: If you use Stripe checkout, payment is processed by Stripe under their privacy policy. We receive confirmation of payment to unlock premium features (e.g. hiding ads, higher suggestion limits) on your device via a token stored locally in your browser.
  • Advertising: If enabled, Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies as described in Google’s policies. You can manage ad settings through Google and your browser.

Storage on your device

We may store a small token in your browser’s localStorage to remember premium status. You can remove it by using “Sign out premium” in the app or clearing site data for this domain.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the site operator at support@timezonemeet.app.
