TimeZoneMeet FAQ

Help with timezone lookup, premium, and sign-in restore.

How do I find the local time in a city?

Type a city name, choose a suggestion, and press Lookup. The app shows the current local time and timezone for the matched city.

Why are city suggestions limited?

Suggestion limits keep lookup fast and fair for all users. Free mode has a lower cap. Premium raises that cap.

What does premium unlock?

Premium removes ads and increases the maximum suggestion limit.

How do I restore premium on another device?

Open Sign in, enter the same email you used when purchasing premium, then use the one-time sign-in link we send by email.

I did not receive my sign-in email. What should I do?

Check Spam/Junk and Promotions, then search for TimeZoneMeet sign-in link. If needed, request a new link because each one expires in 20 minutes.

How accurate are timezone results?

TimeZoneMeet uses city coordinates and timezone lookup logic to map cities to timezone identifiers. If a city has multiple variants, choose the closest suggestion by country.
